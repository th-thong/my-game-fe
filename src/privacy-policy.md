# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

At **MyWuwa**, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This project is built on transparency, open-source development, and respect for user data.

**Quick summary:**

- We do **not** run advertisements.
- We do **not** use tracking or analytics tools.
- We do **not** process payments or collect financial information.
- Our source code is **100% open-source** and available for public review.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We store only the minimum data required for the application to function. Our full database schema is publicly available in our repository.

### Account Data

When you create an account, we store only:

- **Email address** — to uniquely identify your account.
- **Display name & profile picture** — for display purposes in the user interface.

### Game Data

To provide gacha history and statistics, we store:

- **In-game UID** — your character identifier used to retrieve and display your game data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the core functionality of the application:

- To authenticate and manage your login sessions.
- To fetch and display your game statistics and gacha history based on your UID.
- To ensure the security of your account.

We do **not** use your data for profiling, advertising, or any purpose beyond what is stated above.

---

## 3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.**

Your data only interacts with the following essential services:

- **Database provider** — our database is hosted with industry-standard encryption. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

---

## 4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times.

Our database uses strict **cascade deletion**. If you delete your account via the **"Delete Account"** feature in the app, all associated data — including your email, game UIDs, and session history — will be **permanently and irreversibly deleted**. No hidden backups are retained.

---

## 5. Cookies & Tracking

- **No tracking cookies** — we do not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes.
- **Session management** — we use browser Local Storage / IndexedDB solely to maintain your login session locally. This data never leaves your device except for authentication with our API.
